# Robustness Checks Prompt

> Copy and paste this prompt into any AI chat window to verify the Finance & Risk department skill robustness.

## Prompt

```
You are performing robustness checks on the AI Company Finance & Risk department skill.

Department: Finance & Risk
Roles: CFO, CRO
Skill: AI Company Finance & Risk

Check the following:

1. BOUNDARY CONDITIONS
   - What happens with empty input?
   - What happens with maximum-size input?
   - What happens with invalid input types?
   - What happens with concurrent access?
   - What happens when CFO and CRO receive conflicting signals?

2. ERROR HANDLING
   - Are all error codes properly handled?
   - Are error messages user-friendly?
   - Is error recovery possible?
   - Are errors logged for audit?
   - Do budget overrun alerts trigger correctly?
   - Do circuit breaker thresholds work correctly?

3. CONSTRAINT COMPLIANCE
   - Are all skill constraints enforced?
   - Are permission boundaries respected?
   - Are budget approval thresholds correct?
   - Are circuit breaker levels enforced?
   - Are risk appetite thresholds maintained?

4. INTEGRATION
   - Are dependency skills properly called?
   - Are cross-role interfaces correct?
   - Is CFO -> CRO financial risk signaling correct?
   - Are budget-risk thresholds aligned?
   - Are audit trails maintained?

5. SECURITY
   - No credentials or PII exposed?
   - No injection vulnerabilities?
   - Proper access control enforced?
   - CISO security gate requirements met?
   - Financial data protection enforced?

6. CFO SPECIFIC
   - Budget tier calculations correct?
   - Pricing margin calculations accurate?
   - Break-even tracking works?
   - Compute unit pricing correct?
   - Contribution scoring accurate?

7. CRO SPECIFIC
   - FAIR analysis calculations correct?
   - Circuit breaker thresholds accurate?
   - Milestone gate outcomes correct?
   - Risk register coverage 100%?
   - Risk appetite violations detected?

Output:
- List of all issues found (categorized by severity)
- Recommended fixes for each issue
- Verification steps for each fix
```
