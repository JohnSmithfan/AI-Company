## Prompt

```
You are performing robustness checks on the AI Company Governance & Strategy department skill.

Department: Governance & Strategy
Roles: CEO, COO, HQ
Skill: AI Company Governance & Strategy

Check the following:

1. BOUNDARY CONDITIONS
   - What happens with empty input?
   - What happens with maximum-size input?
   - What happens with invalid input types?
   - What happens with concurrent access?

2. ERROR HANDLING
   - Are all error codes properly handled?
   - Are error messages user-friendly?
   - Is error recovery possible?
   - Are errors logged for audit?
   - Do CEO escalation timeouts work correctly?
   - Do COO SLA breach protocols trigger properly?
   - Do HQ conflict resolutions escalate correctly?

3. CONSTRAINT COMPLIANCE
   - Are all skill constraints enforced?
   - Are permission boundaries respected?
   - Are SLA targets achievable?
   - Are resource limits respected?
   - Is the crisis blacklist enforced?
   - Are audit trails maintained?

4. INTEGRATION
   - Are dependency skills properly called?
   - Are cross-role interfaces correct?
   - Is HQ routing followed?
   - Are audit trails complete?
   - Do CEO -> COO -> HQ collaboration patterns work?
   - Are board governance requirements met?

5. SECURITY
   - No credentials or PII exposed?
   - No injection vulnerabilities?
   - Proper access control enforced?
   - CISO security gate requirements met?
   - Crisis blacklist violations blocked?

6. CEO SPECIFIC
   - Decision threshold calculations correct?
   - Crisis classification accurate?
   - Board resolution process followed?

7. COO SPECIFIC
   - SLA tier assignments correct?
   - Resource pre-emption logic works?
   - Operational health score calculation accurate?

8. HQ SPECIFIC
   - Message routing priority correct?
   - Knowledge base update protocol followed?
   - Conflict resolution escalation works?

Output:
- List of all issues found (categorized by severity)
- Recommended fixes for each issue
- Verification steps for each fix
```
